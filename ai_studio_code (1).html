Frequently Asked Questions

How long does visa processing take?

Processing times vary by destination. Typically, UK visas take 2-4 weeks, Canada takes 4-12 weeks, and US appointment wait times fluctuate. We will provide an exact timeline during your consultation.

Do you guarantee visa approval?

No genuine agency can "guarantee" a visa, as final decisions lie with the embassy. However, we guarantee a flawless, highly optimized application that maximizes your chances and avoids automatic rejections due to errors.

Are there hidden charges?

Absolutely not. We believe in 100% transparency. During your consultation, you will receive a complete breakdown of our service fees, embassy fees, and required financial proofs.
